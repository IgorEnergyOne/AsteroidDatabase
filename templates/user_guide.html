Parameters and Their Explanations

Parameter Explanation
Number The number of an asteroid.
Name The name of an asteroid.
Provisional designation The alphanumeric code indicating the half-month of discovery and the sequence within that half-month.
Orbital class 
The class of an object based on its orbit:
    MBA - Main Belt Asteroid (2 au < a < 3.2 au, q > 1.66 au)
    MCA - Mars Crossing Asteroid (a < 3.2 au, 1.3 au < q < 1.66 au)
    OMB - Outer Main Belt Asteroid (3.2 au < a < 4.6 au)
a Semi-major axis of the orbit of an asteroid in astronomical units (AU)
e Eccentricity of the orbit of an asteroid (from 0 to 1)
i Inclination of the orbit of an asteroid (degrees)
H The absolute magnitude of an asteroid
Diameter The effective size of an asteroid in kilometers (km)
Diameter Error The uncertainty of the asteroid's diameter
Albedo The geometric albedo of an asteroid.
Albedo Error The uncertainty of the asteroid's albedo
Taxonomy class An asteroid spectral type is assigned to asteroids based on their reflectance spectrum, color, and albedo.
